Welcome to Paidenia!

Once upon a time...

Deep within the enchanted woods of Paidenia, a delicate balance between Nature and Knowledge sustain the forest's magic. At the heart of this harmony lie the Greenlight Crystals- radiant sources of power connected to the core elements: Earth, Water, and Air. But now... that balance is unraveling. The Pollurors, twisted beings born from ignorance and neglect, are leeching the forest's energy and spreading decay. The Greenlight Crystals are beginning to crack, and with them, the wisdom of math, science, and language is fading. It's up to you to restore the Greenlight Crystals, heal the forest, and bring knowledge back to Paidenia before it's lost forever.
