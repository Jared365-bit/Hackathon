About Us

We are a group of passionate students dedicated to improving elementary education. Studies technology-based learning is 30% more likely to improve students' problem-solving skills. Our Mission

We want to improve comprehension and literacy rates across children ranging from elementary to high schoolers in New Jersey by introducing them to an engaging, fun, and magical game that incorporates questions to reinforce their knowledge across three core subjects.
